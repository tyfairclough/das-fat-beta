Provider information

ACL Essex is an established apprenticeship provider covering the whole of the county. Linked to Essex County Council we offer high quality apprenticeship programmes that meet the needs of local employers.

Our dedicated and professional team deliver programmes up to and including level 5. We can support new employees and help develop existing staff and managers.

Training is bespoke to your business and will include an element of classroom delivery as part of the programme.

All of our delivery staff have industry experience and a wealth of knowledge in their subject area.

We offer the following apprenticeships: Health & Care, Management, Administration, Supporting Teaching & Learning, Customer Service, Childcare, Accounting, Pharmacy and Hair.
