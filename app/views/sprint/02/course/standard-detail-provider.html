About the apprenticeship:

This Advanced Apprenticeship can be applied across a broad range of sectors and job roles. Experienced business administrators are essential to the success of any business, therefore the skills gained with this Advanced Apprenticeship are transferable and valuable to any organisation.

Our team of qualified trainers have at least 10 years' experience in the field of Business and Commerce, this means we can offer role-specific training to meet the needs of your business. We will conduct an in-depth Training Needs Analysis to design a bespoke programme to suite your business needs.

The Business Administration Apprenticeship programme at Level 3 will provide your staff with the opportunity to gain a nationally recognised qualification.

Provider information

Established in 2014 primarily to deliver Apprenticeship programmes, Ensis Solutions Ltd offer a range of Intermediary and Advanced Apprenticeships, Traineeships and work based learning programmes to small and large employers based in; Business & Commerce, Health & Social Care, Logistics, Manufacturing and Construction sectors.

Our programmes are bespoke meeting individual business needs and delivered at their place of work or in locally sourced training suites.

We believe that a combination of our experience, excellent teaching and learning by industry experts and coverage across the south, south east, Midlands and the North of England with a local approach to all our customers sets us apart from our competitors.
